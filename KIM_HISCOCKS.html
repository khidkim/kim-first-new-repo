Kimberley Hiscocks

I am a Graphic Designer and Illustrator based in the Greater Toronto Area with advertising agency, corporate design, and small in-house studio experience.

My interests include typography, print design, web design, collecting ephemera (for inspiration) and continuing education to keep up with the ever changing world of digital design.

I am an experienced user of Adobe CS6 (Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, Acrobat), QuarkXpress, MS Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint), HTML, CSS and WordPress.

I'm a classically trained illustrator and enjoy drawing, painting and digital illustration.
